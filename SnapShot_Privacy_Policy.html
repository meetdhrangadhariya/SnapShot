🔒 Privacy Policy

SnapShot: Full Page Capture & Editor | Last updated: April 2026

Our Core Promise

SnapShot is a 100% local tool. Everything happens on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any of your data — ever.
No accounts required No servers No data collection No tracking

What Data We Access

To capture screenshots, SnapShot temporarily reads the visible content of the current browser tab. This data is:

  • Processed entirely within your browser
  • Never sent to any external server
  • Discarded immediately after you close the editor
  • Never stored beyond your active session

Permissions Explained

  • activeTab — Required to capture the currently open tab you choose to screenshot.
  • scripting — Required to scroll the page and stitch full-page screenshots together.
  • tabCapture — Required to access the tab's media stream for screen recording.
  • downloads — Required to save your exported PNG, JPG, PDF, or WebM file to your Downloads folder.
  • storage — Used only to remember your preferences (e.g. hide-elements toggle) locally.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, location)
  • Browsing history or visited URLs
  • Screenshots or captured images
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Device identifiers or IP addresses
  • Any form of cookies or tracking identifiers

Third-Party Services

SnapShot does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data services. The only third-party library used is jsPDF (an open-source PDF generation library), which runs entirely in your browser and does not transmit data.

Children's Privacy

SnapShot does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The extension has no user accounts and collects no personal data from any user of any age.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the extension constitutes acceptance of any changes.
